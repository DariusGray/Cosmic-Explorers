🛸 Cosmic Explorers Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Across the Galaxy

This Privacy Policy explains how Cosmic Explorers collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

Last updated: 22 April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Cosmic Explorers, we may collect information that you provide directly, including:

  • username
  • email address
  • account login details
  • challenge submissions and mission completion details
  • progress data such as points, unlocked planets, and leaderboard information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your account
  • authenticate your login session
  • display your profile, progress, and leaderboard ranking
  • let you create and complete challenges
  • improve the website’s performance, functionality, and user experience
  • maintain the security and integrity of the platform

3. Local Storage, Cookies, and Similar Technologies

Cosmic Explorers currently uses browser storage such as localStorage to keep certain information on your device, such as your login token, user data, and your cookie/privacy preferences.

We may also use cookies or similar technologies to support essential site functions, improve usability, remember preferences, and analyse website traffic if such features are added in the future.

You can manage your cookie or privacy choice through the consent banner shown on the site. You can also clear your browser storage at any time from your browser settings.

4. Legal Basis and Consent

By using Cosmic Explorers, you acknowledge that certain data may be processed where necessary to provide the website’s core features, such as login, account management, and game progression.

For non-essential storage or tracking features, where applicable, we will request your consent through our privacy or cookie consent notice.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information where reasonably necessary to operate the website, comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, or protect the security of Cosmic Explorers and its users.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as it is needed for account functionality, website operation, security, record-keeping, or legal compliance. If your account is removed, related data may be deleted or anonymised where appropriate.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information against unauthorised access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • request access to your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request deletion of your data
  • withdraw consent for non-essential storage or tracking
  • object to or restrict certain processing activities

To make a request, please contact the site administrator or development team through the appropriate project contact channel.

9. Third-Party Services

If Cosmic Explorers later integrates external tools, embedded content, analytics, authentication providers, or third-party APIs, those services may collect or process data under their own privacy policies.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Cosmic Explorers project team or website administrator through the contact method provided for this project.
